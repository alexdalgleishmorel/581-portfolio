A Tale of Two Buttons

Description

This project describes our group member Carson Bergen. He is someone who greatly enjoys several different hobbies, the most notable of which being music and programming. On top of Carson's hobbies, he also greatly values taking care of his body, in which working-out and eating are essential aspects of his life.

For our project, we wanted to capture all these different aspects of Carson, since no single aspect was more important to him than another. As a result, we came up with the idea of creating quadrants, each of which represents a different portion of his hobbies or priorities. To demonstrate how he constantly transitions between these different interests, we created a figure representative of Carson, which can be dragged and move to different quadrants to allow him to do that activity.

Once Carson's figure has been dragged into a quadrant, an action button allows the user to make him do whichever activity the user has assigned him to. This way of demonstrating the different aspects of who Carson is allows the user to engage in an interactive experience where they must interact with Carson himself to truly reveal all the different elements of his personality.
